Case Study: Basement Reno in South Windsor

May 2025 • Project Spotlight

The Challenge

Our clients, the Smith family in South Windsor, had an unfinished basement that was dark, damp, and wasted space. They wanted a modern family rec room, extra storage, and a guest suite for visiting relatives. Their biggest concerns: water issues, staying on budget, and finishing in time for summer.

Our Solution

  • Full waterproofing (seal cracks, proper drainage, moisture barrier subfloor)
  • Custom framing to maximize usable space
  • Upgraded insulation and soundproofing for year-round comfort
  • Drywall, paint, vinyl plank flooring
  • Built-in storage under stairs and egress window installation for safety
  • Added a 3-piece guest bathroom

The Result

What was once wasted space is now a bright, dry, finished basement: a huge family room, guest suite, laundry area, and loads of storage. All work was done with city permits, inspected, and finished ahead of schedule — under budget.

Project Takeaways

  • Waterproofing and planning early avoids costly headaches.
  • Custom framing lets you use every inch, even in tricky layouts.
  • Work with contractors who handle permits and code for peace of mind.
  • Invest in quality insulation for comfort and lower bills.
