How Much Does a Kitchen Renovation Cost in Windsor?

June 2025 • Cost Guides

Planning a kitchen remodel in Windsor or Essex County? Here’s what you can expect to spend, what drives costs up (or down), and how to get the most for your budget in 2025 — all from local contractors who know the real numbers.

Typical Kitchen Renovation Cost Ranges in Windsor

The cost of a kitchen renovation in Windsor can vary a ton based on size, materials, and the scope of work. Here’s a breakdown of what local homeowners typically spend in 2025:

  • Basic update (keep layout, new finishes): $15,000 – $25,000
  • Mid-range remodel (new cabinets, counters, lighting): $25,000 – $40,000
  • High-end/major reno (layout changes, all new): $40,000 – $80,000+

*Prices above include labour, materials, and typical permits for Windsor, Ontario. Every project is different — contact us for a detailed quote.

What Drives Kitchen Reno Costs?

  • Cabinetry: Stock cabinets cost much less than custom builds.
  • Countertops: Laminate, quartz, granite, or butcher block — each has a different price point.
  • Flooring: Tile, hardwood, vinyl plank, or refinishing existing floors affects costs.
  • Layout Changes: Moving walls or changing plumbing/electrical adds expense.
  • Finishes & Fixtures: Faucets, hardware, and lighting can be budget or premium.
  • Permits & Inspections: Required for layout changes or moving plumbing/electrical in Windsor.

How to Budget & Where to Save

  • Keep the same kitchen layout to save on plumbing and electrical.
  • Mix high-end and budget finishes (splurge on countertops, save on hardware).
  • Consider painting existing cabinets for a lower-cost refresh.
  • Do demo or painting yourself — but leave skilled trades to pros for safety & permits.
  • Ask about off-season or multi-project discounts (we offer these!).

FAQs

How long does a kitchen reno usually take?

2–6 weeks, depending on the scope. Custom cabinetry or layout changes take longer.

Do I need permits for a kitchen remodel?

Yes — in Windsor, you need permits if moving plumbing, electrical, or making structural changes. We’ll handle this for you.

Can I live at home during the renovation?

Usually yes, but there may be periods where your kitchen is off-limits. We’ll work to minimize disruption.
