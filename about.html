About GHD Build Co.

GHD Build Co. is a local Windsor-based crew delivering honest, quality carpentry and renovations across Essex County. We’re a small team of tradesmen — not a faceless franchise — and take pride in work that lasts. You’ll deal directly with the crew doing your job, from first quote to final sweep-up.

  • Licensed & insured for your peace of mind
  • Worksite is always clean, safe, and family-friendly
  • All projects done to code — permits, inspections, and city rules included
  • Clear, written estimates and honest pricing

We also work with trusted, local partners for plumbing, electrical, painting, and flooring — so you get a full-service renovation with one point of contact.
