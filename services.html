Our Services

From decks and basements to full renovations, GHD Build Co. delivers clean, code-compliant work with local expertise and trusted trade partners for any home or business project.

Decks & Fencing

Custom pressure-treated or composite decks and fences, with permits and professional finishing. We design, build, and repair outdoor spaces for year-round use.

Basement Renovations

Transform your basement into a rec room, rental unit, or home office. Full-service finishing: waterproofing, insulation, drywall, flooring, and egress windows.

Trim & Finish Carpentry

Detailed baseboards, crown molding, wainscoting, feature walls, custom door and window trim, and cabinetry installation.

Interior Renovations

Open up your space or refresh tired rooms—walls, ceilings, floors, doors, painting, and more. We coordinate trusted partners for plumbing, electrical, flooring, and paint as needed.

Small Projects & Repairs

From laundry room upgrades to stair repairs, we handle jobs big or small—always with attention to detail.

More Than Just Carpentry

Through our network of trusted local pros, GHD Build Co. can coordinate full-service renovations:

  • Flooring installation
  • Plumbing & fixture upgrades
  • Painting (interior & exterior)
  • Electrical work (licensed partners)
